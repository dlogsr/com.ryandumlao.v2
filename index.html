About

A Bay Area native currently in Los Angeles, I have undertaken a variety of experiences throughout my life. From early 2000 web development to electrical engineering research and work, as well as a strong passion for knowledge in both professional and personal interests, I am a well-rounded and experienced engineer.

I hold an MS from UCLA and a BS from UCSD in Electrical Engineering, with emphasis on Solid State Electronics and Photonics. My interests include nanotechnology, photonics, biomedical applications, and the ever-growing tech industry, among many others. I have strived to maintain diverse working opportunities, creative hobbies and an overall exciting life experience.

On the side, I have experience and great enthusiasm for front-end web design; during the first dot-com boom I ran a highly successful website, portal and webforum based around the Pokemon franchise within the IGN.com network. Currently, I enjoy making visually attractive, interactive, and catchy websites along with simplistic and responsive designs. I also dabble on an as-needed basis in the back-end side with PHP and MySQL.

I am continuously expanding my knowledge and skills in both engineering and web development; I also enjoy photography and video editing. I am a versatile, unique and driven individual.

This site is perpetually changing. All elements of this site are self-written using HTML5, CSS and jQuery/JavaScript.
